About Us


Our Vision

Our vision is to create a seamless and efficient way to convert handwritten Nepali text into digital form, making it easily accessible, editable, and searchable. We envision a future where technology seamlessly integrates with everyday tasks, making handwritten text recognition effortless and accurate. By developing cutting-edge solutions, we strive to empower individuals, researchers, and organizations to unlock the potential of handwritten Nepali text in various domains, including education, research, and cultural preservation.


Our Mission


Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning to create a reliable and efficient tool for recognizing and digitizing handwritten Nepali text. We aim to facilitate the transition from paper-based to digital documentation, preserving the rich heritage of the Nepali language and making it accessible to a broader audience.
